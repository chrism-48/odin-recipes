Hamburger Stroganoff

Great Hamburger Stroganoff

I love dishes like this. Easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 rib celery, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1 (10 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 (6 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • ¼ cup white wine
  • ¼ cup sour cream, or more to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, celery, and garlic in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir until beef is browned and crumbly, about 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease, if needed.

  2. Stir cream of mushroom soup, beef broth, mushrooms, and wine into the skillet. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer until stroganoff begins to thicken, 20 to 25 minutes. Add sour cream, salt, and pepper; cook until hot, about 10 minutes.
